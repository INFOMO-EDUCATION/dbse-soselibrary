About DBSE Collection

The Delhi Board of Secondary Education (DBSE) section of our library contains all the essential resources for students following this curriculum. Our collection is carefully curated to support students from Class 1 through Class 12.

We provide:

  • All prescribed textbooks for each class and subject
  • Supplementary study materials and guides
  • Previous years' question papers
  • Model test papers and sample answers
  • Subject-specific reference books

Our DBSE collection is updated annually to ensure alignment with the latest curriculum changes and examination patterns. The materials are organized by class and subject for easy access.

Key Features of Our DBSE Collection

Textbooks

Complete set of prescribed textbooks for all classes (1-12) including:

  • Mathematics
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Social Studies
  • Languages (English, Hindi, Sanskrit)
  • Computer Science

Exam Preparation

Resources to help students excel in board exams:

  • 10 years of solved question papers
  • Model test papers with solutions
  • Chapter-wise important questions
  • Marking scheme and answer writing guides

Supplementary Materials

Additional learning resources:

  • Concept maps and quick revision guides
  • Workbooks with practice problems
  • Laboratory manuals for science subjects
  • Sample projects and assignments
